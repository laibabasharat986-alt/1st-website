2022

Cousins Realationship

Cousins are the unique keepers of our earliest memories, the ones who remember the chaotic holiday dinners, the secret languages of childhood, and the shared laughter that only family can truly understand. They occupy that beautiful middle ground between a sibling and a best friend—close enough to know your every flaw, yet distant enough to offer a fresh perspective when life gets complicated. Even when months or years pass without a word, a single conversation with a cousin can instantly transport you back to being ten years old, proving that no matter how much you change, you’ll always have a piece of home in each other.
